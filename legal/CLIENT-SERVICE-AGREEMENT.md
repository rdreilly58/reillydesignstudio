This Client Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the date of the signed Statement of Work ("Effective Date") by and between:

## 1. SERVICES

Studio agrees to perform the design services described in the Statement of Work ("SOW") attached to this Agreement. Each SOW is incorporated herein by reference. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and any SOW, the SOW controls.

Studio will perform all services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards.

## 2. FEES AND PAYMENT

### 2.1 Project Fees
Fees are set forth in the applicable SOW. Studio requires a **50% deposit** of the total project fee before work begins. The remaining balance is due upon delivery of final files or as otherwise specified in the SOW.

### 2.2 Invoicing
Studio will issue invoices via email. Payment is due within **14 days** of the invoice date unless otherwise agreed in the SOW.

### 2.3 Late Payments
Invoices unpaid after 14 days accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (18% per year). Studio reserves the right to suspend all work and withhold delivery of files until all outstanding balances are paid in full.

### 2.4 Expenses
Client agrees to reimburse Studio for pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses (stock photography, fonts, printing, travel) within 14 days of invoicing. Expenses exceeding $100 require written approval before being incurred.

### 2.5 Taxes
Fees do not include applicable taxes. Client is responsible for any sales, use, or similar taxes arising from the services.

## 3. REVISIONS

### 3.1 Included Revisions
Each SOW specifies the number of revision rounds included. A "revision round" means one consolidated set of changes communicated in a single feedback document.

### 3.2 Additional Revisions
Revisions beyond those included in the SOW are billed at Studio's current hourly rate (specified in the SOW), invoiced monthly.

### 3.3 Scope Changes
Changes that materially alter the scope, timeline, or deliverables of a project ("Change Orders") must be agreed upon in writing and may result in additional fees and timeline adjustments.

## 4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 4.1 Work Product
Upon receipt of full payment, Studio assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the final deliverables created specifically for Client under the SOW, including all applicable copyright. This assignment does not extend to preliminary concepts, drafts, or rejected designs.

### 4.2 Studio Tools and Pre-Existing Work
Studio retains all rights to its tools, processes, methods, templates, and any pre-existing intellectual property used in the creation of deliverables. Client receives a non-exclusive, perpetual license to use any pre-existing elements incorporated into the final deliverables.

### 4.3 Third-Party Assets
Client is responsible for obtaining licenses for stock photography, fonts, and other third-party assets unless Studio's SOW explicitly includes sourcing them. Studio will notify Client of any third-party assets used.

### 4.4 Portfolio Use
Studio reserves the right to display the final work in its portfolio, on its website, and in promotional materials, unless Client requests confidentiality in writing prior to project commencement.

### 4.5 Credits
Client agrees to credit Studio when reasonably practical (e.g., website footer: "Design by Reilly Design Studio").

## 5. TIMELINE AND DELIVERY

### 5.1 Project Timeline
Estimated timelines are set forth in the SOW. Timelines are contingent upon Client providing timely feedback, approvals, and materials.

### 5.2 Client Delays
If Client fails to provide required materials or feedback within **7 business days** of a request, Studio may adjust the project timeline accordingly. If Client-caused delays exceed **30 days**, Studio may treat the project as abandoned (see Section 8.3).

### 5.3 File Delivery
Final files will be delivered digitally upon receipt of full payment. Studio will retain project files for **90 days** after delivery. Archival storage beyond 90 days may be requested at an additional fee.

## 6. CLIENT RESPONSIBILITIES

Client agrees to:

- Provide all necessary materials, content, assets, and information in a timely manner
- Designate a single point of contact with authority to provide feedback and approvals
- Review and approve work within the timeframes specified in the SOW
- Ensure all materials provided to Studio do not infringe third-party rights
- Obtain necessary permissions for any content, logos, or trademarks provided

Client represents and warrants that it has full authority to enter into this Agreement and that its use of the deliverables will not infringe any third-party rights.

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Each party agrees to keep confidential any non-public information disclosed by the other party ("Confidential Information") and to use it solely to perform obligations under this Agreement. This obligation does not apply to information that is publicly available, independently developed, or required to be disclosed by law.

This obligation survives termination of this Agreement for **3 years**.

## 8. TERMINATION

### 8.1 Termination for Convenience (Client)
Client may terminate this Agreement at any time with **14 days written notice**. Client owes payment for all work completed through the termination date, plus a **kill fee** equal to 25% of the remaining unpaid project balance.

### 8.2 Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate immediately if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within **7 business days** of written notice.

### 8.3 Abandonment
If Client fails to respond or provide materials for **30 consecutive days**, Studio may treat the project as abandoned. Client owes payment for all work completed. Studio retains all rights to work-in-progress materials.

### 8.4 Effect of Termination
Upon termination, Client's rights to work-in-progress materials are limited to the amount paid. Full intellectual property rights transfer only upon payment in full (as per Section 4.1).
